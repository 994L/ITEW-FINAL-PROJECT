Welcome to Cabuyao City!

Cabuyao City, located in the province of Laguna, Philippines, is known for its rich history and cultural heritage. The city offers various tourist attractions such as:

Historical Landmarks

The city is home to several historical landmarks, including the St. Polycarp Parish Church, which dates back to the Spanish colonial period.



Natural Attractions

Cabuyao boasts beautiful natural attractions, such as lakes and parks, where visitors can relax and enjoy the scenic beauty.

Why Visit Cabuyao?

  • Rich cultural and historical heritage
  • Beautiful natural landscapes
  • Vibrant local festivals and events
  • Warm and welcoming community
