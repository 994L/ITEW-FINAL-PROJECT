About Us

Welcome to Cabuyao City Travel and Tour! We are your premier travel and tour agency located in the heart of Cabuyao City, Laguna. Our mission is to provide you with unforgettable travel experiences that showcase the beauty, culture, and history of Cabuyao and its surrounding areas.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Cabuyao City Travel and Tour started with a passion for travel and a desire to share the hidden gems of our city with the world. Our founders, lifelong residents of Cabuyao, wanted to create a company that not only provides exceptional tour services but also promotes local tourism and supports the community.

Our Team

Our team is composed of experienced travel professionals, local guides, and customer service experts who are dedicated to ensuring your trip is seamless and enjoyable. We pride ourselves on our personalized service and attention to detail, making sure every aspect of your journey is taken care of.

What We Offer

  • Guided City Tours
  • Cultural and Historical Tours
  • Adventure and Nature Trips
  • Customized Travel Packages
  • Corporate and Group Travel Services

Our Commitment

At Cabuyao City Travel and Tour, we are committed to providing high-quality travel experiences that are both enjoyable and educational. We believe in responsible tourism that respects the environment and the local communities we visit. Join us on a journey to explore the wonders of Cabuyao City!
